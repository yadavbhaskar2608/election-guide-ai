📋 Step by Step

Your Complete Voting Guide

Follow these simple steps to cast your vote and make your voice heard.

Step 01
📝

Register as a Voter

Every Indian citizen aged 18+ can register as a voter. Submit Form 6 online at voters.eci.gov.in or visit your local BLO (Booth Level Officer).

✅ Age must be 18+ years
✅ Must be Indian citizen
✅ Register in your constituency
Register Now →
Step 02
🔍

Check Your Name in Voter List

Before election day, verify your name in the official voter list using your name, EPIC number, or mobile number.

✅ Visit voters.eci.gov.in
✅ Use Voter Helpline: 1950
✅ Download Voter Slip
Search Voter List →
Step 03
🏛️

Visit Your Polling Booth

On election day, visit your designated polling booth. Booths are usually open from 7:00 AM to 6:00 PM. Carry your Voter ID or any approved photo ID.

✅ Bring Voter ID / Aadhaar
✅ Reach early to avoid queues
✅ Check booth location online
Find Your Booth →
Step 04
🗳️

Vote Using EVM

A polling officer will verify your identity. You'll be given a slip to proceed to the EVM. Press the button next to your chosen candidate.

✅ Your vote is secret & safe
✅ VVPAT shows your vote
✅ You can press NOTA if needed
Vote Confidentially 🔒
📄

📅 Election Phases

Election Timeline

Understand the complete journey of an Indian General Election from announcement to results.

📢
Phase 1

Election Announcement

Model Code of Conduct Begins

The Election Commission of India (ECI) announces the election schedule. The Model Code of Conduct (MCC) comes into effect immediately.

📋 MCC Active 📰 Date Announced 🗂️ Nominations Open
📣
Phase 2

Nomination & Campaign Period

~4–6 Weeks Before Polling

Candidates file nomination papers. Parties campaign across constituencies. Rallies run until the 48-hour campaign silence period before voting.

🏃 Candidates File 📢 Rallies & Debates 🔇 48hr Silence
🗳️
Phase 3

Voting Day (Poll Day)

7:00 AM – 6:00 PM

Eligible voters visit their polling booths and cast votes on EVMs. VVPAT lets you verify your vote. NOTA is available as an option.

⏰ 7AM–6PM 🔒 Secret Ballot 🛡️ Security Deployed
📊
Phase 4

Vote Counting & Result Day

After Final Phase of Polling

EVM seals are checked and counting begins. The party with a majority (272+ seats) forms the government and its leader becomes Prime Minister.

🔢 Counting Begins 🏆 Winner Declared 🏛️ Govt. Formation

💡 Did You Know?

🗳️

India has the world's largest election with over 96 crore registered voters.

⚡

India pioneered the use of Electronic Voting Machines (EVMs) on a large scale.

❌

NOTA (None of the Above) was introduced in 2013, giving voters the right to reject all candidates.

📍

Remote polling booths are set up even in forests and mountains to ensure no voter is left behind.
