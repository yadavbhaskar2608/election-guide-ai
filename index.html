📋 Step by Step

Your Complete Voting Guide

Follow these simple steps to cast your vote and make your voice heard.

Step 01
📝

Register as a Voter

Every Indian citizen aged 18+ can register as a voter. You need to submit Form 6 online at voters.eci.gov.in or visit your local BLO (Booth Level Officer).

✅ Age must be 18+ years
✅ Must be Indian citizen
✅ Register in your constituency
Register Now →
Step 02
🔍

Check Your Name in Voter List

Before election day, always verify your name and details in the official voter list. You can check it online using your name, EPIC number, or mobile number.

✅ Visit voters.eci.gov.in
✅ Use Voter Helpline: 1950
✅ Download Voter Slip
Search Voter List →
Step 03
🏛️

Visit Your Polling Booth

On election day, visit your designated polling booth. Polling booths are usually open from 7:00 AM to 6:00 PM. Carry your Voter ID or any approved photo ID.

✅ Bring Voter ID / Aadhaar
✅ Reach early to avoid queues
✅ Check booth location online
Find Your Booth →
Step 04
🗳️

Vote Using EVM

Inside the polling booth, a polling officer will verify your identity. You'll be given a slip to proceed to the EVM (Electronic Voting Machine). Press the button next to your chosen candidate.

✅ Your vote is secret & safe
✅ VVPAT shows your vote
✅ You can press NOTA if needed
Vote Confidentially 🔒
📄

📅 Election Phases

Election Timeline

Understand the complete journey of an Indian General Election from announcement to results.

📢
Phase 1

Election Announcement

Model Code of Conduct Begins

The Election Commission of India (ECI) announces the election schedule. The Model Code of Conduct (MCC) comes into effect immediately. Political parties cannot make new promises or use government resources for campaigning.

📋 MCC Active 📰 Date Announced 🗂️ Nominations Open
📣
Phase 2

Nomination & Campaign Period

~4–6 Weeks Before Polling

Candidates file their nomination papers. After scrutiny, the final list is published. Candidates and parties campaign across constituencies. Rallies, speeches, and advertisements run until the campaign silence period (48 hours before voting).

🏃 Candidates File 📢 Rallies & Debates 🔇 48hr Silence
🗳️
Phase 3

Voting Day (Poll Day)

7:00 AM – 6:00 PM

Eligible voters visit their polling booths and cast votes on EVMs. VVPAT (Voter Verified Paper Audit Trail) lets you verify your vote. Security forces are deployed to ensure free and fair elections. NOTA is available as an option.

⏰ 7AM–6PM 🔒 Secret Ballot 🛡️ Security Deployed
📊
Phase 4

Vote Counting & Result Day

After Final Phase of Polling

EVM seals are checked, and counting begins under strict supervision. Results are declared constituency-wise. The party or alliance with a majority (272+ seats in Lok Sabha) forms the government and its leader becomes the Prime Minister.

🔢 Counting Begins 🏆 Winner Declared 🏛️ Govt. Formation

💡 Did You Know?

🗳️

India has the world's largest election with over 96 crore registered voters.

⚡

India pioneered the use of Electronic Voting Machines (EVMs) on a large scale.

❌

NOTA (None of the Above) was introduced in 2013, giving voters the right to reject all candidates.

📍

Remote polling booths are set up even in forests and mountains to ensure no voter is left behind.
